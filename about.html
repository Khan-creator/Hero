Learn About Us

South Sudan's non-profit organization

  • About
  • Mission
  • Vision
HERO supports the States Government structures at County, Payam, Boma, facility, and community levels in counties and States in concert with national level strategies and investments to offer the highest quality essential services including integration of HIV, TB, Family Planning and Reproductive Health (FP/RH), Malaria, Maternal, Neonatal and Child Health (MNCH), nutrition, Food security and Livelihood, Education, Protection-GBV and child Protection, advocacy, Peace building, Water, and sanitation Health (WASH), ES/NFIs, Climate Change interventions – while addressing the social determinants of health. The project has integrated innovative interventions for effective service delivery through public, NNGO, INGO, FBO, and for-profit private sectors. The project continues to actively promote synergy between Counties and community level service delivery, States and county level cluster coordination, and national level technical assistance components. The project has strengthened for delivery and in the following results which it still be achieves:
To create conscious society Devoid of conflict & suffering.
To encourage society to adopt peaceful co-existence and embraces integrity as a way of life through innovative awareness and outreach programs that build bridge of hope and encourage equitable sharing of available resource.
